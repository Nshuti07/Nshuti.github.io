Nshuti Kevin Mugabo

Network & Communication Specialist • UI/UX Designer

Hello! I’m Nshuti Kevin Mugabo, a Network & Communication Specialist and UI/UX Designer with a strong foundation in both technical infrastructure and creative design.

I bring hands-on experience in network design, configuration and management — working with TCP/IP, routing & switching, VLANs, DHCP, DNS, firewalls, VPNs, and system security. I’m experienced with network simulation and tools such as Cisco Packet Tracer and have configured devices, subnets and access controls for reliable, secure networks.

On the front-end side I build accessible interfaces using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and responsive frameworks. I combine monitoring, performance tuning and security best-practices with clear UI to create systems that are efficient and easy to use.

Fluent in English, French and Kinyarwanda, I collaborate well across teams and design with learners and end-users in mind.
